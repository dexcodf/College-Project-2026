# Acme Corp — Shipping Policy

## Processing time
Orders are processed within 1 business day. Orders placed after 4:00 PM local
time are processed the next business day.

## Delivery estimates
- **Standard shipping:** 3–5 business days. Free on orders over $50.
- **Express shipping:** 1–2 business days. Flat fee of $12.
- **International shipping:** 7–14 business days, calculated at checkout.

## Tracking
A tracking number is emailed once your order ships. You can also view tracking
under Account → Orders.

## Lost or delayed packages
If a package has not arrived within 10 business days of the estimate, contact
support and we will open a carrier investigation or reship at no cost.
