# Acme Corp — Returns & Refunds

## Return window
Most items can be returned within **30 days** of delivery for a full refund.
Items must be unused and in original packaging.

## Non-returnable items
Gift cards, downloadable software, and personalized items are not eligible.

## How to start a return
1. Go to Account → Orders and select the item.
2. Choose "Return" and print the prepaid label.
3. Drop the package at any partner carrier location.

## Refund processing
Refunds are issued to the original payment method within **5–7 business days**
after we receive the returned item. You will get an email confirmation.

## Damaged or defective items
If an item arrives damaged, contact support within 48 hours with a photo and we
will ship a replacement immediately at no charge.
