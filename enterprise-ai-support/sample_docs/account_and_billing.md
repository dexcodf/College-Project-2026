# Acme Corp — Account & Billing

## Resetting your password
Open Settings → Security and choose "Reset password". A confirmation link is
emailed to you and is valid for 30 minutes.

## Updating payment methods
Go to Account → Billing to add or remove cards. The default card is used for all
future charges and renewals.

## Subscriptions
- Plans renew automatically on the billing date shown in Account → Billing.
- Cancel any time before the renewal date to avoid the next charge.
- Cancelled plans remain active until the end of the paid period.

## Invoices
Downloadable PDF invoices are available under Account → Billing → Invoices for
the last 24 months.

## Contacting a human agent
Type "talk to an agent" in chat, or email support@acme.example. Human agents are
online Monday–Friday, 9am–6pm.
