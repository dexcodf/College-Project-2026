# Acme Corp — Product Warranty

## Coverage
All Acme hardware products include a **24-month limited warranty** covering
defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

## What is covered
- Manufacturing defects
- Component failure not caused by misuse
- Battery capacity dropping below 70% within the warranty period

## What is not covered
- Accidental damage, liquid damage, and cosmetic wear
- Damage from unauthorized repairs or modifications

## Making a claim
Contact support with your order number and a description of the issue. Approved
claims are repaired or replaced within 7–10 business days. Shipping for warranty
claims is covered by Acme.

## Extended warranty
An optional **AcmeCare+** plan extends coverage to 36 months and adds one
accidental-damage incident per year.
